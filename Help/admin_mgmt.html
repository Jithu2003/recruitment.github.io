Administrator Accounts [ Help Index ]

The Administrator is the person who manages the system in order to function efficiently so that the maximum output can be achieved.

The System consists of the Administrator Area where the Candidate Selection and the Exam Management is managed. The system allows to create a group of Administrators which could work in co-ordination to manage the exam and the recruitment process. With the Super Administrator on the top to control all the other Administrators, the system helps to group the Administrators according to their Designation and their Access Rights. The system allows not more than 100 Administrators in the system including the SuperAdministrator.

The Designation is specified according to the Access Rights granted. So the Designation divides the Administrators into different types according to their Access rights.

The SuperAdministrator is the Administrator who cannot be deleted and has unrestricted Access Rights. While the other Administrators specified in the Designation act as the group of Administrators who function at various levels below the SuperAdministrator to manage the system efficiently.
