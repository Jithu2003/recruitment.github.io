Designation [ Help Index ]

The Designation is the position of the Administrator among the group of Administrators who manage the system. It not only indicates the Access Rights assigned to the Administrator but also the priority or the level of management assigned to the Administrator.

The Designation is binded with the Access Rights which can be modified only by the Super Administrator in the Accessibility Options. Every Administrator is assigned with a Designation by the Super Administrator when creating the Administrator and can be modified by the Super Administrator in the Accessibility Options.

There are Designation is specified according to the Access Rights granted. So the Designation divides the Administrators into different types according to their Access rights.

Super Administrator : Super Administrator is the Administrator who cannot be deleted and has unrestricted Access Rights.
